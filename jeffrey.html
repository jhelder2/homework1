A Little About Myself

My Name is Jeffrey Helder, born December 21st 1996. I've been off school now for almost five years.
I have worked as an apartment building manager, first year automotive apprentice, and most recently
was a labourer/forklift operator at a warehouse in Cambridge. I've been slowly learning the basics to
the film/animation pipeline, 3D design, figure drawing, marketing/advertising and basic art skills over
the past two years. My goals and admiration in life is to be a better designer than I can imagine.
I want to work primarily with 3D design because I see a huge pull towards it in today/tomorrow's
market, but would like to be multi-talented with front and backhouse applications. The end goal (after
many years of learning, struggling, and crying) is to operate my own studio. As much as I don't like
it because it’s high risk, competitive, and incredibly hard to get into, the fire to my passion is
entertainment. Animation esspecally.

I'm back in school now because I got tired of telling myself;
“you have the rest of your life to follow your dreams”.
